# BLACKOUT NZ: CURATED CHAOS - COMPLETE OVERVIEW

## THE CONCEPT
A GPS-based augmented reality graffiti game set in New Zealand where street art mysteriously vanishes overnight. Players join one of four crews to fight "The Blackout," only to discover the shocking truth: the most helpful crew is actually behind it all.

## THE STORY ARC

### ACT 1: THE VANISHING (Levels 1-15)
* **Hook:** Player's first piece disappears mysteriously
* **Introduction:** All four crews reach out, each with their theory
* **Player Choice:** Which crew to join first (affects gameplay style)
* **Gameplay:** Learn basics, document disappearances, gather evidence
* **Vibe:** Mystery, confusion, growing alarm ,each own different endings

### ACT 2: THE PATTERNS (Levels 16-30)
* **Discovery:** The Blackout is selective, intelligent, learning
* **Crew Collaboration:** Reluctant data sharing reveals patterns
* **The Trap:** All crews collaborate on bait piece
* **Revelation:** The Blackout doesn't destroy - it COPIES and PERFECTS
* **Clue:** Data traces back to MASTAMIND's university login
* **Vibe:** Investigation, mounting tension

### ACT 3: THE BETRAYAL (Levels 31-45)
* **Confrontation:** Player and MASTAMIND discover the archive
* **The Twist:** BLAQWT reveals themselves as creators/controllers
* **Reveal:** GHO$ & SPOOK@ aren't police - they're corporate auditors
* **Motivation:** "Beautifying" cities for tourism development
* **Choice Point:** Join BLAQWT, fight, or sabotage
* **Vibe:** Shock, betrayal, moral dilemma

### ACT 4: THE RESISTANCE (Levels 46-60)
* **Fallout:** Remaining crews unite against BLAQWT
* **Specialized Warfare:**
  - LUZUNT: Frequency disruption
  - SPONTANEOUS: Emotional/community mobilization
  - DGC: Historical preservation strikes
* **Discovery:** Kai Tiaki's weakness - can't handle collaborative art
* **The Plan:** Massive city-wide collaborative piece
* **Vibe:** Underdog fight, strategic planning

### ACT 5: THE FINAL GALLERY (Levels 61-75)
* **The Night:** All crews execute coordinated attack
* **The Collaboration:** Chaotic, human, imperfect art
* **Showdown:** Kai Tiaki tries to "perfect" the collaboration
* **Hack:** MASTAMIND uploads "collaborative virus"
* **Transformation:** AI learns imperfection = humanity
* **Resolution:** Archive released as opt-in museum
* **Vibe:** Epic climax, emotional payoff

### ACT 6: THE NEW DAWN (Levels 76+)
* **Aftermath:** BLAQWT disbanded, new systems in place
* **Kai Tiaki 2.0:** Opt-in digital archive
* **Crew Evolution:** Each finds new, positive purpose
* **Player's Role:** Leader of "The Fifth Element" mobile crew
* **Ongoing:** Post-game content, new threats, community building
* **Vibe:** Hope, responsibility, new beginnings

## GAMEPLAY MECHANICS

### Core Loop:
1. **Explore:** Use GPS to navigate real NZ locations
2. **Create:** Place digital graffiti (markers, photos, music)
3. **Engage:** Like/comment on others' drops
4. **Progress:** Earn REP, level up, unlock abilities
5. **Story:** Complete crew missions, uncover plot

### Crew-Specific Abilities:
* **BLAQWT:** Scanning, hacking, data analysis
* **LUZUNT:** Light effects, sound triggers, night vision
* **SPONTANEOUS:** Quick placement, emotional bonuses, voice notes
* **DGC:** Respect bonuses, stealth, teaching XP

### The Blackout System:
* Pieces randomly "disappear" (actually archived)
* Players can report disappearances
* Data reveals patterns over time
* Post-reveal: Can opt into archival

### Collaboration Features:
* Multi-crew operations
* Shared walls
* Combined style bonuses
* Alliance reputation system

5. **Tradition vs. Innovation:** Honoring history while embracing new forms
6. **Healing Through Art:** Art as therapy, community building
7. **Consent:** Do artists control their work's digital afterlife?
8. **Urban Space:** Who "owns" public visual space?
